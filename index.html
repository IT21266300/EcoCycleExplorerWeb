Project Scope

Literature Survey

The EcoCycle Explorer project aims to enhance sustainable bicycle tourism in Sri Lanka by leveraging AI-driven technologies. Extensive research highlights the importance of integrating AI in tourism to provide safer, more personalized, and environmentally friendly experiences. For instance, studies by Fernando et al. demonstrate how machine learning algorithms can optimize cycling routes, tailoring them to user preferences and safety requirements. Similarly, research by Perera and Silva emphasizes the role of real-time data in improving traveler decision-making and satisfaction through personalized recommendations.

Recent advancements in mobile and AI technologies have further revolutionized tourism experiences. In the context of Sri Lanka, integrating AI with mobile applications can address critical challenges such as real-time hazard detection, health monitoring, and navigation. The EcoCycle Explorer system aims to be a comprehensive solution for cyclists, enhancing their travel experience by providing dynamic, safe, and culturally immersive routes tailored to seasonal variations and user preferences. Learn more about our approach in the literature review.

Research Gap

The EcoCycle Explorer project addresses the following research gaps:

Seasonal Route Optimization

Existing systems often fail to consider seasonal variations in route planning. The EcoCycle Explorer system integrates real-time weather data and user preferences to recommend optimal cycling routes, promoting safer and more enjoyable travel experiences across diverse climates and terrains.

Localized Route Recommendations

Traditional travel apps often neglect localized insights. EcoCycle Explorer incorporates AI to provide culturally rich and environmentally sensitive routes. This feature supports the discovery of hidden landmarks and promotes interaction with local businesses, enhancing the overall tourist experience.

Real-Time Hazard Detection

Safety is a major concern for cyclists. Existing technologies lack real-time hazard detection capabilities. The EcoCycle Explorer system employs AI-powered hazard detection to identify road conditions, warn cyclists of potential dangers, and suggest safer alternative routes dynamically.

Location Based Voice Navigation System

To identify the research gap, this study compares the features and objectives of the proposed location-based voice navigation system for bicycle tourists in Sri Lanka with existing systems and research. While prior studies have significantly advanced navigation technology, smart tourism, and voice-controlled systems, they often fail to address the specific needs of bicycle tourists in Sri Lanka.

Research Problem & Solution

Addressing the challenges and opportunities in Sri Lankan bicycle tourism

Research Problem

How can AI-driven solutions enhance bicycle tourism in Sri Lanka, addressing safety, accessibility, and environmental sustainability?

Sri Lanka's bicycle tourism potential is underutilized, facing challenges such as safety concerns, lack of optimized cycling routes, and limited technological integration. Cyclists often struggle with navigation, seasonal unpredictability, and the absence of real-time hazard detection, which affects their overall experience. Additionally, cultural and eco-tourism opportunities remain untapped, requiring a solution that promotes sustainable tourism while enhancing user satisfaction.

Key Challenges:
Safety concerns
Poor navigation
Limited tech integration
Untapped opportunities

Our Goal

To provide personalized travel planning, real-time assistance, and an enriched tourism experience while promoting environmental sustainability and cultural preservation in Sri Lankan bicycle tourism.

Research Objectives

Develop AI-Powered Route Optimization Algorithms

Create algorithms that optimize bicycle routes based on real-time data, user preferences, and environmental factors. By integrating machine learning models, the system will ensure safe, efficient, and sustainable travel experiences.

Enhance Safety with Real-Time Hazard Detection

Implement AI-driven hazard detection systems to analyze road conditions and provide timely alerts to cyclists. This feature ensures safer navigation and proactive risk management.

Promote Sustainability and Cultural Tourism

Integrate cultural insights and eco-tourism elements into the application. Highlight lesser-known destinations and promote sustainable travel practices to enrich the tourism experience and support local communities.

Sentiment Analysis of Hotel Reviews for Personalized Recommendations

Utilize NLP techniques to analyze user-generated reviews, extracting insights aligned with preferences. The system provides personalized accommodation suggestions (e.g., family-friendly, budget, or luxury) to enhance satisfaction.

Develop a Location-based Voice Navigation System

Offer detailed landmark descriptions via voice navigation, highlighting cultural, historical, and scenic significance to transform travel into an informative and engaging adventure.

Methodology

Our comprehensive approach to building an AI-powered cycling tourism platform

Figure 1. High-Level Architecture of the EcoCycle Explorer System

The EcoCycle Explorer system is architected around four intelligent components that work seamlessly together:

1. Optimal Route Planning

Personalized travel routes generated using advanced machine learning models including KNN and Ant Colony Optimization. Integrates real-time data from Google Maps API for dynamic travel times and cost optimization.

2. Targeted Promotions

AI-driven promotional system leveraging user calendars, seasonal preferences, and NLP models to deliver personalized destination recommendations and real-time activity notifications.

3. Real-Time Hazard Detection

Safety is a major concern for cyclists. Existing technologies lack real-time hazard detection capabilities. The EcoCycle Explorer system employs AI-powered hazard detection to identify road conditions, warn cyclists of potential dangers, and suggest safer alternative routes dynamically.

4. Sentiment-Based Personalization

Advanced NLP-powered sentiment analysis of hotel reviews and user feedback to provide highly personalized accommodation suggestions that align with individual preferences.
