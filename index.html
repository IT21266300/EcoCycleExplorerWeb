Explore Features

Innovative Features

Discover how our AI-powered platform revolutionizes cycling tourism in Sri Lanka

Smart Route Optimization

AI algorithms analyze terrain, weather, and safety data to create personalized cycling routes that adapt to seasonal variations and your preferences.

Real-time Hazard Detection

Advanced AI monitoring system identifies road conditions, weather hazards, and obstacles, providing instant alerts and alternative route suggestions.

Cultural Discovery

Uncover hidden landmarks and local gems with AI-curated cultural insights that promote sustainable interaction with local communities.

Voice Navigation

Location-based voice guidance system with real-time directions, landmark recognition, and cultural storytelling for an immersive experience.

Sentiment Analysis

NLP-powered analysis of hotel and destination reviews provides personalized accommodation recommendations based on traveler preferences.

Eco-Sustainability

Promote sustainable tourism practices while supporting local businesses and minimizing environmental impact through responsible travel planning.
